Fair Work first

Set the award before you add shifts

  1. Open the official Fair Work Award Finder and confirm the award, classification, employment type, and rate date.
  2. Copy the final hourly rates into the day, evening, overnight, weekend, and public-holiday fields in Salary Setup.
  3. Add your public holiday dates, then enter each shift with the date, time on, time off, and unpaid break minutes.

Overnight shifts are split around midnight automatically. Holiday dates and timed holiday windows are applied minute by minute so the public-holiday rate flows through the right hours of the shift.
